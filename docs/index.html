Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Personal Data and Storage

Our QR Scanner app is built with privacy-first principles. We do NOT collect, harvest, or transmit any personal information. All scanned data, barcodes, formats, and timestamps are saved exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. This history never leaves your device and is fully controlled by you. You can wipe your history at any time using the "Clear All" action in the app.

2. Device Permissions

The app requests the following camera permission:

  • Camera Access: Required to capture frames and read barcodes or QR codes through the camera. Camera frames are processed on-device in real-time and are never recorded or stored.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools to help analyze app stability and display advertisements:

  • Google Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Used to track anonymous usage behavior and report app crashes so we can fix bugs quickly. No personally identifiable data is shared.
  • Google AdMob: Serves standard banners and interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect user data such as advertising identifiers to personalize ads.

4. Contact Details

If you have any questions or feedback regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at our GitHub support workspace.
